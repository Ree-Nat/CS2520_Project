How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Objective

Be the first player to get 3 of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

How to Play

  • Player X goes first
  • Players take turns clicking on an empty cell
  • Place your mark (X or O) in the cell you click
  • The game alternates between X and O automatically
  • Click New Game to start over

Winning

You win by getting 3 of your marks in a row:

  • Horizontally - across any row
  • Vertically - down any column
  • Diagonally - from corner to corner

Tie Game

If all 9 cells are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game ends in a tie.
